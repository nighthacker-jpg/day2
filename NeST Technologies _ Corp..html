About

NeST Technologies is a global, privately held, enterprise-level IT company founded
in the late 1990s. We design, develop, test, and implement software solutions and deliver
a diverse suite of industry-specific back-end services to Fortune 100 and small-to-midsize
clients in banking and finance, healthcare, insurance, aerospace and other important
vertical markets.

About NeST Group of Companies

NeST Technologies Corp., is a division of the The NeST Group of Companies. 

Headquartered in Sterling, Virginia, the NeST Group is a global technology firm with assets in a diverse number of industries, including software, electronics manufacturing, healthcare, fiber optics, the Internet of Things (IoT), etc. 

Globally, NeST employs more than 4,000 employees through locations in the United States, Europe, the Middle East, India, Japan and Australia.
